Organizations

Rescue Me

Most adoption sites help you find cats and dogs. Some will help you find reptiles and farm animals. Rescue Me goes even further and not only has countless pets up for adoption, but also has resources available for wildlife rehabilitators. Given the variety of animals available, Rescue Me requires future pet parents to narrow down their choice beforehand. Once you've decided on the type of pet you want to adopt, you can narrow your search down by state.

Home To Home

Home To Home™ is an online pet placement platform that helps pet owners find new homes (permanent or temporary) for their pets. Home To Home™ believes pets that need a new home do best if they can be transitioned directly from their current home to a new, loving home and avoid the stress of a stay at an animal shelter

Petfinder

Petfinder specializes in all kinds of pets, from the traditional dogs and cats to barnyard buddies like horses, bunnies, and pigs. You can filter your pet search by age, organization, and traits, and receive endless advice about training and grooming.

Voz animal Peru

"voz animal Peru" is a Peruvian organization that is dedicated to rescuing dogs and cats in critical life or death situations to cure them, sterilize them and give them up for responsible adoption. They also have a volunteer program for all those interested in helping to give a second chance for rescued animals.
