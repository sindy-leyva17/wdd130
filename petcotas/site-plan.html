Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to raise awareness among people about animal adoption and encourage them to adopt. To achieve this, I am going to speak from my own experience.

Audience

Most of my audience will be people interesting in adopting animals, like parents, people that live alone and couples. I believe that I can make my website attractive, for people interesting in adopting, using striking colors and fonts.

Content

Home page

My website home page has the objective to help people that are looking for information about adopting an animal from my own experience. These people can have some questions about adopting that I can help answer, for example: why is adopting an animal good? What do I need before adopting an animal? How can I adopt an animal? It is ok if I adopt an animal for my child? and so on. Adopting an animal is not something to take lightly, it is better obtaining all the possible information before taking the decision to be responsible for a life. Once the person interesting in adopting has all her or his doubts resolve, it is probable that starts to wonder, where he or she can adopt a pet? Being someone who has adopted more than once and having more than one friend or family member who has also adopted, I consider that my knowledge will be a good base of information for a future adopter. I can give future adopters ideas about where to adopt a pet and how to prepare the house to receive them. Having a pet is a bless and for that reason we need to be responsible.

Organizations

Although my experience as an animal adopter and the experience of my acquaintances can be a good source of information, we are not experts. The second page of my website is intended to share information about some organizations that deal with animal adoption. There are many different organizations around the world and many of them can handle different types of animal adoption, from the most common species such as dogs and cats to not so common ones such as oxen, snakes, and poisonous spiders. Organizations can also work in a specific country, for this reason I intend to share information about organizations that work here in my country and some that work in other countries such as the United States, Canada, and so on. By offering a global perspective, my goal is to provide valuable help to a broader audience of people interested in embarking on the noble journey of adoption. However, in the future I intend to expand the information from organizations around the world to make the adoption of more animals possible. Thanks to the organizations of animal adoption all over the world more people and animals can give and have second opportunities, and that is absolutely awesome.

Fun facts

The third section of my website is dedicated to the fascinating world of animal trivia and fun facts. While my personal experience and connections have provided me with valuable information about animal adoption, my passion extends to sharing the delightful aspects and often quirky things from the animal kingdom. This segment will reveal a variety of captivating tidbits about our furry, feathered, and scaly friends. From the intriguing behaviors of dolphins to the quirky habits of squirrels, these fun facts will not only entertain but also deepen our appreciation for the diversity and wonders of "The Animal World. "Join me as we explore the lighter side of the animal kingdom, as we discover these delights that celebrate the magic of our animal companions. As you could well read, this section has no other purpose than to help us get to know a little more about those who cannot communicate with us through words but perhaps with attitudes or gestures that often tend to mean, I love you, you are the best, I'm hungry or many other things. In this world of constant change, especially for the animal world, I consider that it is our responsibility to learn from those who every day make their greatest effort to be able to survive.
